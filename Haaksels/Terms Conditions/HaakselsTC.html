  • Distance Contract: A contract concluded between Laura's Haaksels and the consumer as part of an organized system for distance selling of products and/or services, using one or more means of distance communication up to the contract's conclusion.
  • Means of Distance Communication: A method that can be used for concluding a contract, without the consumer and Laura's Haaksels being in the same place simultaneously.
  • Reflection Period: The period during which the consumer can exercise their right of withdrawal.

  • Remote Contract: A contract formed between the entrepreneur and the consumer as part of an organized system for the remote sale of products, digital content, and/or services, where, up to and including the contract's conclusion, exclusive or partial use is made of one or more techniques for distance communication.
  • Standard Withdrawal Form: The European model withdrawal form included in Annex I of these conditions. Annex I does not have to be provided if the consumer has no right of withdrawal regarding their order.
